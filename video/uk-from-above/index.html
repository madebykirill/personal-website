UK From Above

Produced by:Kirill Zeynalov

Genre:Travel Documentary

Release Date:2020-2021

In this series, Kirill takes us on a breathtaking aerial journey across the UK, exploring four iconic cities from a unique perspective. Episode 1 kicks off in Southampton, capturing its historic waterfront and vibrant culture. In Episode 2, we soar over Manchester, showcasing its mix of industrial heritage and modern architecture. Episode 3 brings us to Bournemouth, where stunning coastlines meet charming townscapes. Finally, Episode 4 takes us above Cardiff, revealing the city's blend of historic landmarks and lush green spaces. Experience the UK like never before, from above!
