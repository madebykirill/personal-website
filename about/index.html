About Me

Hi, I am Kirill Zeynalov — a London-based motion designer, videographer and content creator.

I was 12 when I picked up my first camera and began making short films. With the dream to create audiovisual content I moved to London, where I earned a Bachelor's Degree in Music Production and Sound Engineering followed by a Master's Degree in Accessible Filmmaking.

Since then I have been developing my passion for visual art and explored new ways of creative storytelling: 2D animation, augmented reality and sound design.

By this time I have been involved in multiple projects combining expert knowledge with creative thinking. Gradually learning essential techniques ranging from directing and video editing to music production and graphic design, I have been working with creative people providing industry-standard solutions across audio, video and visual art fields.
