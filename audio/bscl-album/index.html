Between The Stars And City Lights

Composed and Mixed by:Kirill Zeynalov

Genre:Ambient, Hybrid, Cinematic, Post-Rock

Release Date:19.04.2026

This album is about my childhood. It's a collection of memories - quiet mornings, places I remember, and moments that stayed with me over the years. A lot of this music comes from that time - from being young, from family, from small things that felt big back then. Some of it is clear, some of it is blurred, like memory usually is.

This album is my way of going back for a moment and remembering everything that made me who I am.
